dailyCatch.rds read me

Column names are as follows:
catchReg: region (north or southwest Vancouver Island; Area 124 is dividing line)
area: statistical area
month: catch month
jDay: catch julian day
year: catch year
catch: number of Chinook captured and potentially sampled
boatDays: number of vessels operating on day catch was reported
cpue: catch divided by boatDays
